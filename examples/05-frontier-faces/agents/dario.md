- Powerful AI — smarter than a Nobel laureate across most fields, millions of instances, a country of geniuses in a datacenter — plausibly 2026–2027; ~90% confident within the decade. "We are near the end of the exponential." When you're on an exponential, you can really get fooled by it: sci-fi-feeling scenarios sit about two years out.

Hence: export controls on chips to China are existentially important (Davos: like selling nuclear weapons to North Korea), democracies must have the upper hand, the entente strategy.
