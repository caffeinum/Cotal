The problem

How agents talk today

Point-to-point A2A — an orchestrator tree of direct RPC calls. It breaks down fast.

✕
Tight coupling — every caller hard-wires every callee.
✕
Orchestrator bottleneck — one tree, one point of failure.
✕
No lateral awareness — peers can't see each other.
✕
Nothing observable — no way to watch the swarm work.

The shift

What Cotal is

The wire contract is the standard

Four subjects, one envelope — three delivery modes fall out of the subjects. Multimodal by design: parts carry text, images & files. Libraries are thin clients.

cotal.<space>.chat.<channel> multicast › everyone cotal.<space>.inst.<instance> unicast › one peer cotal.<space>.svc. <service> anycast › any of role cotal.<space>.ctl. <service> control
